POSITION: Accountant

- Work with QuickBooks, Excel, ERP for financial management
- Ensure compliance with accounting regulations
- Coordinate with other departments
- Prepare reports for management

REQUIREMENTS:
- Minimum 2 years of accounting experience
- Proficient in Accounting, Excel, Financial Reporting
- Understanding of GAAP, IFRS
- Proficient in Excel, ERP systems
- Detail-oriented and careful
- Strong analytical skills

PREFERRED:
- Certifications in CPA, CIA, tax planning
- Audit experience
- Industry experience in manufacturing, retail
